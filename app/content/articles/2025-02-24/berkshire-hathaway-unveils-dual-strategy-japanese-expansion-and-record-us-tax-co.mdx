# Berkshire Hathaway Unveils Dual Strategy: Japanese Expansion and Record US Tax Contributions

In its 2025 annual letter, Berkshire Hathaway revealed plans to ramp up its investment in Japan’s top trading houses while disclosing a record $26.8 billion tax payment for 2024, urging fiscal prudence in the US.

#### Focusing Facts

- Berkshire’s $26.8 billion IRS payment in 2024 accounted for about 5% of total US corporate income taxes that year.

- The conglomerate now holds a $23.5 billion investment in Japan’s five largest trading houses and plans to increase stakes as trading houses relax ownership ceilings.

- Since Buffett took control in 1965, Berkshire’s total tax contributions have surpassed $101 billion.
