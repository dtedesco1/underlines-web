# Google Unveils Gemini 2.5 Pro AI with Integrated Reasoning and Multimodal Prowess

Between March 26 and 28, 2025, Google introduced its experimental Gemini 2.5 Pro—a thinking AI model that reasons through responses, excels in coding and multimodal tasks, and pushes the envelope in context understanding.

#### Focusing Facts

- Gemini 2.5 Pro topped the LMArena leaderboard as of March 28, 2025.

- The model features a 1 million token context window, with plans to extend to 2 million tokens.

- Initial deployment is on Google AI Studio and the Gemini app, with Vertex AI integration pending and pricing details forthcoming.
