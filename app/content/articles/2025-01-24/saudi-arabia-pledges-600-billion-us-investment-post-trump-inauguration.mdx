# Saudi Arabia Pledges $600 Billion US Investment Post-Trump Inauguration

Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman declared a plan for Saudi Arabia to invest $600 billion in the US over the next four years in a call with President Trump.

#### Focusing Facts

- The $600 billion investment will be directed towards expanding US-Saudi trade and investments over four years.

- This announcement followed Trump's recent re-inauguration and a potential Saudi incentive for hosting Trump's first foreign trip again.

- Historically, Trump's first foreign trip as president deviated from tradition by prioritizing Saudi Arabia over closer allies.
