# Getty Images and Shutterstock Finalize $3.7 Billion Merger Amid AI Competition

Getty Images announces a $3.7 billion merger with Shutterstock to enhance AI capabilities and strengthen market position, pending regulatory approval.

#### Focusing Facts

- Getty Images and Shutterstock will merge creating a $3.7 billion company with Getty shareholders owning 54.7% and Shutterstock 45.3%.

- The merger is anticipated to deliver $200 million in cost savings three years post-completion.

- Getty and Shutterstock shares soared, with Getty up 39.7% and Shutterstock up 22.7% following the merger announcement.
