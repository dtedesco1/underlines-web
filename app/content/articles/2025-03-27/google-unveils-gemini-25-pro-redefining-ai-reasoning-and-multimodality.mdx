# Google Unveils Gemini 2.5 Pro, Redefining AI Reasoning and Multimodality

On March 26-27, 2025, Google launched Gemini 2.5 Pro—a breakthrough reasoning AI with a 1-million-token context—integrated within its Gemini Advanced subscription, marking a significant upgrade in performance benchmarks and enterprise capabilities.

#### Focusing Facts

- Gemini 2.5 Pro scored 18.8% on Humanity’s Last Exam and 63.8% on SWE-Bench, outpacing rivals like OpenAI and Anthropic in key reasoning and coding tests.

- Gemini Advanced, priced around $19.99-$20/month, offers users a 1M token (750,000-word) context window and early access to experimental features including advanced coding and multimodal capabilities.

- Additional updates include Gemini Live’s new video and screen-sharing functions and deep integration with Google Workspace apps like Drive, expanding practical use cases.
