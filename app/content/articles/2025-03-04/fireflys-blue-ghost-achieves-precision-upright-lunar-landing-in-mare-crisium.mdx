# Firefly’s Blue Ghost Achieves Precision Upright Lunar Landing in Mare Crisium

On March 2, 2025, Firefly Aerospace’s Blue Ghost successfully executed a fully controlled, upright soft landing on the Moon, marking a major milestone in commercial space exploration.

#### Focusing Facts

- Landed within 100 meters of its target near Mons Latreille in Mare Crisium.

- Carried 10 NASA-sponsored experiments under a $101 million CLPS contract for a 14-day mission.

- Launched on January 15, 2025 aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9, traversing 2.8 million miles over 45 days with multiple hazard avoidance maneuvers.

This event mirrors the transformative leap seen with the Apollo missions, such as Apollo 17’s crewed landing in 1972, but under a new paradigm where private companies are rapidly advancing space technology.
