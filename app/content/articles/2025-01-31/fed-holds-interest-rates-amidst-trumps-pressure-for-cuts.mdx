# Fed Holds Interest Rates Amidst Trump's Pressure for Cuts

The Federal Reserve decided to hold interest rates steady at 4.25%-4.5%, amid pressure from President Donald Trump to reduce rates.

#### Focusing Facts

- The interest rate decision marks the fourth consecutive Fed meeting without a rate cut, holding rates at 4.25%-4.5%.

- President Trump criticized Fed Chair Jerome Powell and the central bank for not cutting rates, blaming them for persistent inflation.

- Donald Trump promised not to reappoint Powell as Fed Chair when his term ends in 2026.
