# OpenAI Launches GPT-4.1 Suite: Extended Context, Enhanced Coding, and Cost Efficiency

On April 14, 2025, OpenAI unveiled GPT-4.1 and its mini and nano variants, which deliver a 1-million-token context window, improved coding accuracy and instruction following, and lower operational costs compared to previous models.

#### Focusing Facts

- GPT-4.1 models process up to 1 million tokens, achieve 21%-27% improved coding benchmark scores, and are 26% cheaper than GPT-4o for median queries.

- The new models are available exclusively via the API, leading to the planned deprecation of GPT-4.5 preview by July 2025 and omitting the customary safety report.

- User-deployed cost structures, such as a 75% discount on prompt caching, reflect targeted adjustments to bolster real-world application uptake.
