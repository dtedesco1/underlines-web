# Israel-Egypt Exchange Ceasefire Proposals Amid Intensified Gaza Offensive

Between April 11-14, 2025, draft ceasefire documents including hostage-release terms were exchanged as Israel escalated its military operations, completing key corridors and ordering mass evacuations in Gaza.

#### Focusing Facts

- On April 11, Israeli and Egyptian negotiators exchanged draft ceasefire proposals requiring the release of hostages in return for a 40-70-day truce.

- The IDF completed the 'Morag Corridor' in southern Gaza, isolating Rafah and prompting sweeping forced displacement orders across Khan Yunis and surrounding areas.

- Over 250 former Mossad operatives publicly urged Netanyahu to prioritize hostage recovery over renewed military aggression on April 14.
