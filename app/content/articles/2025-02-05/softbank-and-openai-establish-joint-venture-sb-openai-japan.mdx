# SoftBank and OpenAI Establish Joint Venture 'SB OpenAI Japan'

SoftBank Group and OpenAI announced the creation of a 50-50 joint venture named SB OpenAI Japan to expand AI services among Japanese enterprises.

#### Focusing Facts

- SoftBank will spend $3 billion annually to implement OpenAI technology across its companies.

- OpenAI is valued at $300 billion, about the same as TikTok owner ByteDance.

- The venture aims to serve as a model for global AI adoption in the business sector.
